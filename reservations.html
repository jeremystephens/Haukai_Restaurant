Booking Information

Haukai is open from 5pm Wednesday to Sunday Please allow at least two hours for dining with us. For reservations seated at 5pm, we require the table to be returned to us by 7:30pm for the next seating.

Cancellations

If you wish to cancel your reservation, we require 72 hours notice or a cancellation fee of $75 per person will apply.

If you need to cancel your reservation, you may do so by following the link in the confirmation email you receive, by emailing info@haukai.co.nz.

Dietary Requirements

Our current menu is able to accommodate most dietary requirements but please specify all food allergies and dietary requirements when making your reservation. Unfortunately, not all dietary requirements can be catered to.

Private Events

Haukai is available for private events outside of our normal operating hours. Please email info@haukai.co.nz for more information.
