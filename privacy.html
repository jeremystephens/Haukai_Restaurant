Privacy Statement

Introduction

At Haukai Restaurant, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers and site visitors. This privacy statement details the personal data Haukai Restaurant collects, and how we process and use that data.

Information Collection and Use

We collect personal data when you visit our website, make a reservation, or subscribe to our newsletter. This data may include your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you provide. We use this information to manage your reservations, send you updates about our restaurant, and improve our services.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Haukai Restaurant does not sell or rent personal data to third parties. We may share your information with service providers who assist us in our operations, such as reservation management. Any third-party service providers are strictly prohibited from using your personal data for any other purposes.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data that we collect. You can also opt-out of receiving our newsletters at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of our emails.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised or unlawful processing and against accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

Changes to this Privacy Statement

We may update this Privacy Statement from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Statement on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Statement periodically for any changes.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Statement, please contact us at info@haukai.co.nz
